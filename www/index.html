Build an Orb probe image

Pick a supported device, provide an Orb Deployment Token and a root password, and get a ready-to-flash OpenWrt image with Orb baked in. See GOALS.md for what orb-forge is and isn't.

Advanced: additional uci-defaults

Optional. Appended after the recipe's device-specific uci-defaults and the orb-forge common pre-amble. Runs once on first boot. Remember that networking is not yet up when uci-defaults runs.
